HOW TO PLAY

1
SET YOUR GOAL

Enter a starting balance and a target balance goal — or just pick a challenge from the list. You can also hit "Just Play" to skip the goal entirely.

2
HOLD TO BUY · RELEASE TO SELL

Press and hold anywhere on the chart to open a long position. Release to close it and lock in your profit or loss. You can only hold one position at a time.

3
SURVIVE THE ROUND

Each round lasts 30 seconds. Your balance carries over between rounds. Try to grow it as much as possible before the time runs out.

4
REACH YOUR GOAL

If you set a balance goal, your progress is shown at all times. Hit 100% and you'll get a celebration. The GDP challenges are... ambitious.

💡 Pro tip: Don't just hold the whole time. Wait for a dip, buy the bottom, sell the peak. Easy to say, hard to do.
